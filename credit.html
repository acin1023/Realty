FAQs

Credit repair can help improve your score and increase your financial opportunities.
  • -You may need credit repair if you have a low credit score, have been denied credit or loans, or have noticed errors or inaccuracies on your credit report.

  • -The length of time it takes to repair your credit can vary depending on the extent of the damage and the strategies used to improve your score. It could take anywhere from a few weeks to several months or even years to see significant improvements in your credit score.
